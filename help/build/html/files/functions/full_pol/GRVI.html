GRVIÂ¶

Generalized volume Radar Vegetation Index for full-pol SAR data.

\[\text{GRVI} = \left(1 - \text{GD}_{\text{GV}}\right)\Big(\frac{p}{q}\Big)^{2\,\text{GD}_{\text{GV}}}, \quad 0\le \text{GRVI} \le 1\]

where, \(\text{GD}_{\text{GV}}\) is the geodesic distance between Kennaugh \((\mathbf{K})\) matrices of the observed and the generalized volume scattering model, \(p,q\) are minimum and maximum value of distances between \(\mathbf{K}\) matrices of the observed and elementary targets respectively. A detailed explanation of GRVI is available in~cite{ratha2019generalized}
